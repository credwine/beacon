Privacy Is Not Optional. It's the Foundation.

Beacon is built on a radical idea: your personal information should never leave your device. Every analysis, every document you check, every question you ask -- it all happens locally on your computer using Gemma 4, Google's open AI model.

Works Offline

No internet required after initial setup. Perfect for areas with limited connectivity.

No Cloud Storage

Your data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server, ever.

No Account Required

No sign-up, no login, no tracking. Just open and use.

Fully Open Source

Every line of code is public. Audit it yourself. Trust, but verify.

How Trusted Contact Alerts Work
1. You scan a message using the Scam Scanner as normal.
2. Beacon detects danger -- if the trust score is 20 or below (DANGEROUS), an alert is triggered automatically.
3. Your contacts are notified -- an alert record is created with the scam details. In a future update, this will send SMS or email notifications to your trusted contacts.
4. Everything stays local -- alert records are stored on your device only. No data is sent to any cloud service.
